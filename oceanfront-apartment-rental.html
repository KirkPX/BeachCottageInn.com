Maine Oceanview Lodging

Rooms & Rates

The Maine Beach House Suites are a unique oceanfront property located in Lincolnville Beach, a small fishing village in the heart of the Mid-Coast Region of Maine.

We have a variety of accommodations ranging from large, fully-equipped two bedroom suites, to smaller studio suites with kitchenettes. In addition we have adjacent en-suites that can be rented separately or can be added to the larger units to make two-bedroom apartments. We are central to village activity, only steps away from the ever popular sand beach, shops, restaurants and Isleboro Island Ferry. Experience the fun of a rural village and beach atmosphere at your doorstep. Our Beach House Suites are located directly on the ocean with magnificent views of Penobscot Bay and its marine activities.


Maine Oceanfront Apartments VIEW PHOTOS

Just a stone's throw from the water and a few seconds walk from Lincolnville Beach, these well appointed two-bedroom units come equipped with full kitchen, washer/dryer, Satelite TV, free wifi, living room with dining area, AC in the bedrooms, and fresh ocean breezes. Best suited for longer stays, our oceanfront apartments can be rented weekly or longer. Please inquire for monthly rates.
