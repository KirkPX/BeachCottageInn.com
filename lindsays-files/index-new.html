Enjoy the view from our Maine oceanfront accommodations

The view from our ocean front suites offers a panorama of morning sunrises over Lincolnville Beach. With the mid-coast Maine island of Islesboro in the distance you'll be able to watch lobster boats ply Penobscot Bay for their fresh catch of the day. A few seconds walk will take you to the town pier, the ever popular public sand beaches and village shops. Three well known restaurants are the main focus of lunch and dinner activities. The Beach Cottage Inn and Beach House Suites are spacious, well appointed, very comfortable, with large decks that are ideally located right on the ocean. Come and join us, we are eager to share our coast of Maine with you.
