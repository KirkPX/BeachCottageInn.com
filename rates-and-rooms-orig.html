Maine Oceanview Lodging

Rooms & Rates

The Maine Beach House Suites are a unique oceanfront property located in Lincolnville Beach, a small fishing village in the heart of the Mid-Coast Region of Maine.

We have a variety of accommodations ranging from large, fully-equipped two bedroom apartments, to smaller one bedroom and studio suites with kitchenettes. Some rooms are adjacent to one another joined to accommodate larger parties. We are central to village activity, only steps away from the ever popular sand beach, shops, restaurants and Isleboro Island Ferry. Experience the fun of a rural village and beach atmosphere at your doorstep. Our Beach House Suites are located directly on the ocean with magnificent views of Penobscot Bay and its marine activities.
