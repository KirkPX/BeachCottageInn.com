The view from our oceanfront suites offers a panorama of morning sunrises over Lincolnville Beach. With the island of Islesboro in the distance, you'll be able to watch lobster boats ply Penobscot Bay for their fresh catch of the day.

A few seconds walk will take you to the town pier, the ever popular public sand beaches and village shops. Three well known restaurants are the main focus of lunch and dinner activities. The Beach Cottage Inn and Beach House Suites are spacious, well appointed, very comfortable, with large decks that are ideally located right on the ocean.

Arguably one of Maine's best kept secrets, the village of Lincolnville Beach is just a few minutes drive to Camden, Belfast, and Rockland, providing you with access to these beautiful Maine towns from the comfort of a charming coastal fishing town.

Come and join us, we are eager to share our coast of Maine with you.
