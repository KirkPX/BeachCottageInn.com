Oceanfront Apartments

The Maine Beach House Suites are a unique oceanfront property located in Lincolnville Beach, a small fishing village in the heart of the Mid-Coast Region of Maine.

Just a stone's throw from the water and a few seconds walk from Lincolnville Beach, these well appointed two-bedroom units come equipped with full kitchen, washer/dryer, Satellite TV, free WiFi, living room with dining area, AC in the bedrooms, and fresh ocean breezes.

Upper Deck

Unrestricted ocean views from your living room and deck are complemented by wood accents in the rest of the apartment. The Upper Deck is a spacious, fully furnished, two-bedroom apartment with private entrance that will make you feel right at home.

  • Master bedroom with King bed
  • Second bedroom with Full bed
  • Full kitchen
  • Washer/dryer
  • Living room with dining area
  • Private deck with ocean views
  • Air conditioning in bedrooms
  • TV & free WiFi
