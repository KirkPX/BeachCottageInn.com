Activities & Attractions

Discover the charm of Midcoast Maine from our oceanfront location in Lincolnville Beach. Just minutes from Camden and surrounded by natural beauty, cultural attractions, and outdoor adventures, the Beach Cottage Inn serves as your perfect home base for exploring Maine's coastal treasures.
