Welcome to Beach Cottage Inn

Experience the charm of coastal Maine at our oceanfront inn, located in the heart of Lincolnville Beach.

Our Location

Perfectly situated on the shores of Penobscot Bay, the Beach Cottage Inn offers a unique blend of coastal charm and modern comfort. Just minutes from Camden and surrounded by the natural beauty of Midcoast Maine, we provide the perfect base for your Maine adventure.

Our historic 19th-century property has been lovingly restored to offer modern amenities while maintaining its original character. Each room and apartment provides a comfortable retreat with stunning views of Penobscot Bay.
