Our Rooms & Rates

The Beach Cottage Inn offers a variety of one bedroom and studio suites with kitchenettes for short or longer-term stays. Designed for couples and larger parties alike, many of our rooms can also be joined for added comfort and privacy.

We are central to Lincolnville's beach activity, only steps away from the ever popular sand beach, shops, restaurants and Isleboro Island Ferry. We are located directly on the ocean with magnificent views of Penobscot Bay and its marine activities.
