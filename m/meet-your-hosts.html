Meet Your Hosts

Welcome to Beach Cottage Inn! We're Rob and Dorothee Newcombe, and we've been proud innkeepers here since 2003. Our journey began when we fell in love with this beautiful spot on the Maine coast and decided to make it our home.

"We believe in creating a warm, welcoming environment where our guests can truly relax and experience the magic of coastal Maine. Every day, we strive to make your stay memorable and comfortable."

Originally from England and Germany respectively, we bring our European hospitality traditions to this charming corner of Maine. Our backgrounds in hospitality and property management help us ensure that every aspect of your stay meets the highest standards of comfort and service.

Over the years, we've had the pleasure of welcoming guests from all over the world, many of whom have become dear friends and regular visitors. We take pride in sharing our knowledge of the local area and helping our guests discover the hidden gems of Midcoast Maine.

We look forward to welcoming you,

Rob and Dorothee
