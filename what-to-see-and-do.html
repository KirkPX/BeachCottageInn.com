Activities & Attractions in Midcoast Maine

Discover the charm of Midcoast Maine from our oceanfront location in Lincolnville Beach. Just minutes from Camden and surrounded by natural beauty, cultural attractions, and outdoor adventures, the Beach Cottage Inn serves as your perfect home base for exploring Maine's coastal treasures.

Seasonal Events & Festivals

March Maine Maple Sunday - Visit local sugar houses and experience Maine's maple syrup tradition Maine Maple Sunday
May Camden's Spring Harbor Arts & Books Fair - Local artisans and book vendors showcase their work Harbor Arts & Books
June Camden Classics Cup - Classic yacht racing and maritime celebrations Camden Classics Cup
July North Atlantic Blues Festival - Two days of world-class blues music in downtown Rockland Blues Festival
August Maine Lobster Festival - Five days of seafood, entertainment, and maritime traditions Lobster Festival
September Camden Windjammer Festival - Maritime heritage celebration with tall ships and nautical activities Windjammer Festival
October Camden International Film Festival - Acclaimed documentary film showcase CIFF

Outdoor Recreation

Over 30 miles of scenic hiking trails with panoramic ocean views at Mount Battie and Mount Megunticook Camden Hills State Park
Guided sea kayaking tours exploring Penobscot Bay's islands and marine wildlife Maine Sport Outfitters
Scenic cycling along coastal routes and rural back roads of Midcoast Maine Midcoast Cycling Routes
Sandy beach perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and beachcombing Lincolnville Beach
18-hole golf course with stunning ocean views of Penobscot Bay Northport Golf Club

Indoor Activities & Culture

World-class collection of American art, including works by three generations of Wyeths Farnsworth Art Museum
Historic transportation museum featuring rare aircraft, automobiles, and engines Owls Head Transportation Museum
Maritime history museum with interactive exhibits and local seafaring heritage Penobscot Marine Museum
Historic 1912 movie house showing independent and mainstream films Colonial Theatre
Professional theater company presenting classic and contemporary productions Camden Opera House

Food & Dining Experiences

Award-winning winery offering tastings and tours of their vineyard Cellardoor Winery
Local cheese producer offering tours and tastings of artisanal cheeses State of Maine Cheese
Fresh seafood market with live lobster tanks and local catches McLaughlin's Seafood
Weekly farmers market featuring local produce and artisanal foods Camden Farmers Market

Shopping & Arts

Historic downtown district featuring unique boutiques and galleries Downtown Camden Shops
Maine-made crafts, artwork, and handcrafted items Center for Maine Craft
Antique shops and vintage finds in historic buildings Belfast Antique Trail
Local artist studios and galleries featuring Maine artists Camden Art Walk

Maritime Activities

Day sails and sunset cruises aboard historic windjammers Maine Windjammer Cruises
Guided tours to local lighthouses and coastal photography spots Lighthouse Tours
Whale watching excursions to spot humpbacks, finbacks, and marine life Maine Whale Watch
Traditional wooden boat building workshops and demonstrations WoodenBoat School
