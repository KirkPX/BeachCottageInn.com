Day Trips & Activities

Enjoy day trips to the nearby picturesque villages of Castine, Stonington, Spruce Head and Port Clyde. Winter activities include skiing at the Snowbowl, Tobogganing at the Snow Bowl or Cross Country Skiing at Tanglewood (Camden Hills State Park).
